Our Purpose

THE MISSION DECODED.

"Every frame is a masterpiece. Every costume a second skin. We exist to tell that story."

We exist at the intersection of artistry and fandom, documenting the explosion of anime culture and cosplay craftsmanship as they reshape entertainment worldwide. NEON EDITORIAL was founded on a single conviction: niche cultures deserve world-class journalism.


Our editors are not observers — we are participants, creators, and enthusiasts who bring authentic insider perspectives to every story we tell.

How We Got Here

OUR
STORY.

2020
Origin

The Spark

Four freelancers — a journalist, a cosplayer, a photographer, and a developer — shared a blog post that unexpectedly reached 100,000 readers overnight. The Electric Curators were born.

2021
Launch

Neon Editorial Goes Live

Official launch of the platform with a full editorial team, partnering with Anime Expo and Tokyo International Anime Fair for exclusive media access.

2022
Expansion

Global Reach

Built community hubs in Tokyo, Seoul, and Los Angeles. Launched the Neon Podcast with 5M+ streams in its first season and partnered with 40+ independent studios.

2024
Now

The Electric Future

50,000+ subscribers. 18 countries. Still independent. Still obsessed. We're just getting started — the next chapter of niche media is ours to write.
